About us

Locally owned, honestly run, and proud of it.

Essex County Garage Cleaning is a family-run cleanout service based in Windsor, Ontario. We started with one truck, a push broom, and a simple promise: show up on time, work hard, and leave every garage better than we found it.

Built on hard work and handshakes.

We're locally owned and operated. Every job is handled by our own crew — no subcontractors, no revolving door of strangers in your driveway. When you call, you get us.

Founded by Sarkis Raffol, Essex County Garage Cleaning grew out of a simple observation: most people want a clean, usable garage — they just don't have the time, the tools, or the will to haul it all themselves. So we do it for them.

We're fully insured, properly licensed, and we handle disposal the right way — including eco-friendly drop-off for donations, e-waste, and recyclables wherever possible. No illegal dumping, no "we'll figure it out later." Just honest work done well.
